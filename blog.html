Another Entry

Mountains are cool right. Its one of the earths great gifts to humanity.

The fact that we can percieve beauty is astounding. Somewhere along humanities growth we evolved the capability to experience beauty. Why? Is it evolutionarily beneficial to our survival or was it just something that we gained that didn't hurt us in the log run.

Maby it had something to do with finding a mate but if so why then do we view things such as mountains to be so astonishing to us. All it is is a collection of matter. Theoretically this hunk of rock should be no more beautiful to you than your floor at home. Yet people seek out these locations.
